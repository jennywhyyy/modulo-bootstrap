Will there ever be a Shrek 5?

Despite 'Shrek 4' having been released back in 2010, Dreamworks hinted that another film was possible.

So far, so good! Eddie Murphy confirmed that a script had already been written by screenwriter Michael McCullers, who told The Hollywood Reporter in March 2017 that his story was a "pretty big reinvention" for the series, prompting speculation that Shrek 5 could actually be a reboot.
